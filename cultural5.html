Page 4

We are all surrounded by music in our everyday lives. Music has always played a huge role in my life. I don't listen to the cleanest music by any means but I feel like I handle the lyrics and the message a little bit better then others in our society. Music inspires people and makes you feel certain ways, but it doesn’t always have a positive outlook on your life. As I stated many times, music changes the actions and decisions of people in both positive and negative ways, but mostly negative in our society. It comes from that maturity level that I talked about earlier. You never truly know when someone matures, and if they are grown up enough to listen to suggestive material. I stand in the middle because of the way I view music and lyrics is positive, but it gives out a misconception on the life of so many others. YOLO, Miley Cyrus, and Lady Gaga are just three examples of many instances where music lyrics and the meanings behind songs get blown out of proportion and are over used in negative ways. The lyrics based around “twerking” and “yolo” were never to be negative, but the large amounts of immature people take it and turn it into something suggestive. All in all there will always be music and it will always be a booming industry. Our society needs to find a better method of censoring music so those that are immature cant turn non-suggestive material into something negative, as well suggestive material, and how it shouldn't be misused and taken out of context.
