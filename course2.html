Course Retrospective Essay

Being assigned a retrospective assignment really made me think about everything I have learned in this class, and how useful it will be in my future. Getting the chance to stop for a minute and think back on the assignments I have completed in this class during the term was fulfilling. I am confident that this class will forever leave a lasting impact on the rest of my time spent here at Michigan State University. This class is crucial to success. Anyone can write a decent paper and put it in MLA format, but this class separates those that are great and those that still need help.
