Formal Research Paper

Research can come from so many different kinds of sources. It can come from the Internet, the community, suppliers and customers, books and newspapers, and so much more. The most successful companies are the ones using the most up to date research, and moving along with the path of updates in the newest technology of our society. Always looking for the newest and up to date research can make a big difference in your company compared to others.

When someone chooses an academic discipline such as this, it really makes it hard not to do research on methods and others views. You cannot be close-minded while becoming a business major. To be successful you have to very open-minded in learning about new ideas and researching multiple point of views. Having a variety of up to date research ad combining them to create the greatest product ensures great success in the business field.

First let me go in depth on what a business academic discipline necessarily looks like. It is a discipline that comes with rigorous course work and can vary through many different central topics based on the student’s interest. Personally, I have chosen to take the route that is centralized through marketing and advertising. I chose this because of my fathers business and the need of marketers to grow his business. I am open to taking in new ideas, combining what I see in thriving businesses together, using extreme amounts of research, and adding my own personal skills and creativity to my fathers business. If I can do this I will be able to share what I have learned to the marketing team, and I will help the business to continue to grow. Along with researching, writing is also another important factor in business.

Writing is a huge part of the marketing side of business. You need to know how to write and research. Combining both research and writing can show just how strong of a business you have. You cannot have research without writing, nor writing without research in a thriving business setting. Writing is a good way of planning, processing thoughts, combing ideas, and physically being able to take your mental “notes” and put them down on paper (or computer, etc.). An example of this is storyboards, which are a key step in the advertising process. This provides the main purpose of the advertisement in a writing form.
